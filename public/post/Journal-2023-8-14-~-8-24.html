Journal 2023 8-14 ~ 8-24

I've just got up. Everything is still quiet.

I should have started with these habits back in May, especially getting up at 7 in the morning. It gives me what I want: silence, and it also gives me what the night doesn't offer: the rising sun.

My head is suddenly empty.

Bottled water is handy and also tasty. Tap water tastes bad (hard). I cannot bear it.

Let us return to the subject of habits. When do I feel that habits are bullshit and don't want to bother with daily routines? When I am in a hurry. I want the sweetness to come quickly and waiting means failure to me. Mindset changes. One day I asked myself why people choose hardships, is it because they like to endure? Not quite. I realised that hardship promises a future in which we win what we dream of. Foolishly, I thought that fun was the reason people put in the effort, which it is, but for many things the result is the main motivation.

Recently I also learned Javascript. Promise is a key concept in Javascript. In javascript, we often wait for a function to finish and do the next thing only after the function has finished. Waiting takes time, but in the end we get what we want. Waiting also has its advantages: priorities, optimising the use of limited resources... Life is really the same.
