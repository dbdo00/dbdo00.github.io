title: night walk

i walked through campus tonight to reach my goal of 10,000 steps.   
it is brighter than i thought outside. i even fear my small braid (something i never tried wearing outside as a male-presenting person) would be too noticeable .

my patience in walking changes over time. initially i walked a lot and have fun doing it, but then it became less satisfying for me, perhaps because i expected more. but now i can appreciate it better when i don't expect it to be a perfect hobby choice.  
Since i want to walk for a long time, i came to a place which is far but still close to the edge of the campus. the lighting for the paths is dark there, but the court nearby is bright. i saw some old buildings, and weird stairs everywhere. i don't remeber how many stairs i went through just to walk forword. the light was dimmer as i moved forward. i was drawn by the only light around, which came from the entrace of three old buildings to my left. i saw students with long hairs and skirts entered the building together but they seemed not to be talking.


this place wasn't that familiar to me and so i decided to leave. i needed some time to find my way back to the paths i had walked more often and had become more familiar with. but this wasn't so hard. i found a familiar path very soon and i walked on it.
