i walked through campus tonight to reach my goal of 10,000 steps, suprised by how bright it was on the street (the campus is still dark though). i even fear my small braid (something i never tried wearing outside as a male-presenting person) would be too noticeable .

my patience in walking changes over time. initially i walked a lot and had fun doing it, but then it became less satisfying for me, perhaps because i expected more. now i can appreciate it better when i don't expect it to be a perfect hobby choice.  Since i want to walk for a long time, i came to a place which is far but still close to the edge of the campus. the lighting for the paths is dark there, but the court nearby is bright. i saw some old buildings, and weird stairs everywhere. i don't remeber how many stairs i went through just to walk forword. the light was dimmer as i moved forward. the only light around caught my attention, comming from the entrance of three old buildings to my left. i saw students with long hairs and skirts entered one of the buildings together but they seemed not to be talking.


this place wasn't that familiar to me and so i decided to leave. i needed some time to find my way back to the paths i had walked more often and had become more familiar with. but this wasn't so hard. i found a familiar path very soon and i walked on it.
