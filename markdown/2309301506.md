title: night walk

tonight, i walked on campus in order to complete 10,000 steps today. 
it is brighter than i thought outside. i even fear my small braid  would be too noticeable (for a male-looking person).

my patience in walking changes over time. initially i walked a lot and have fun doing it, but then it becomes less statisfying for me, perhaps because i expected more. i found walking is not a snake oil, but i can appreciate it better when i don't expect it to be a perfect hobby choice.
Since i want to walk for a long time, i came to a place which is far but still close to the edge of the campus. the lighting for the paths is dark there, but the court nearby is bright. i saw some old buildings, and weird stairs everywhere. i don't remeber how many stairs i went through just to walk forword. the light was dimmer as i moved forward. the only light around was the light at the entrace of three old buildings. i saw students with long hairs and skirts entered the building together but they seemed not to be talking.


this place is not that familiar to me and so i wanted to leave. i need to think to find my way out of here and go to the paths i walk more often and hence feel more familiar with. this wasn't so hard. i found a familiar path very soon and i walked on it.
